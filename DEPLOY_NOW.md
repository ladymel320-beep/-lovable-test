## ⚡ FASTEST METHOD (2 Minutes):

### Vercel - Drag & Drop Deploy

1. **Go to:** [vercel.com/new](https://vercel.com/new)

2. **Sign up/Login** (free):
   - Use GitHub, Google, or Email
   - No credit card required

3. **Deploy:**
   - Click "Deploy without Git" or "Browse"
   - Select and upload the entire `lovable-site` folder
   - OR upload just the `dist` folder for instant deploy

4. **Wait 30 seconds** - Done! 🎉

**Your live URL will be:** `https://website-builder-express-xyz.vercel.app`

## 🎯 Alternative: Netlify Drop

Even simpler - no account needed initially!

1. **Go to:** [app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
